What is Covered

  • Interior Cleaning: Removal of food particles, splatters, and grease from walls, top, and floor.
  • Turntable & Components: Removal and cleaning of the glass tray, roller ring, and any accessible racks.
  • Exterior Cleaning: Wiping down the exterior body, door, handle, and control panel to remove fingerprints and grease.
  • Odor Removal: Deodorizing the unit to eliminate smells from previous cooking.
  • Eco-Friendly/Non-Toxic Agents: Use of safe cleaning solutions, often steam cleaning methods using water, vinegar, or citrus to break down grime.
  • Inspection: A final check to ensure the appliance is clean and operational.

What is NOT Covered

  • Repairs & Technical Fixes: No mechanical or electrical repairs, such as fixing non-functional keypads, replacing magnetrons, or fixing wiring.
  • Internal Disassembly: Cleaning does not involve opening up the machine to clean internal, hidden components or motor areas.
  • Old/Permanent Stains: While cleaners try their best, there is usually no guarantee that deeply burned, carbonized, or long-term stains will be completely removed, particularly on older appliances.
  • Paint or Structural Damage: Removal of paint stains, cement, or fixing damaged, rusted, or chipped interiors.
  • Over-the-Range Vent Maintenance: While some, like Kaff, mention cleaning filters, many general services exclude in-depth cleaning of, or replacement of, carbon filters and duct pipes.
